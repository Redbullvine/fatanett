Logistics & Shipping

Logistics work under Fatanett focuses on hotshot and regional freight. 369 Logistics LLC and the Goosenecker identity are the core of this segment, with supporting tools being developed to keep loads and communication organized.

Current Logistics Assets

  • Goosenecker — Brand and online presence for gooseneck trucking, imagery, and marketing material around hotshot hauling. goosenecker.com
  • FreightMate — In-progress tool intended to match freight with available trucks and surface the basic information that drivers and shippers actually need.
