App Design & Digital Tools

Fatanett experiments with small, focused applications. Each tool is intended to be practical, quick to use, and capable of standing on its own as a useful utility or early-stage product concept.

Current App Concepts

  • ExitMap — A travel helper designed around exits and stops, focused on keeping drivers aware of what is coming up next on their route. exitmap.app
  • CandyApple — An all-in-one hub concept that brings useful sites, tools, and links into a single place for quick access. candyapple.app
  • Tadaah — A simple “upload and transform” media workflow, turning input photos into AI-assisted output. (Branding and design: black & pink.) tadaah.app
  • FreightMate — A booking and coordination concept aimed at matching freight loads with available hotshot trucks. (In progress on Bubble.)
